Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with HMP?

+

Getting started with HMP is simple:
1. Click the "Get Started" button
2. Choose your role (Teacher or Student)
3. Create your account with basic information
4. Teachers can immediately create courses
5. Students can join courses using course codes
Our intuitive interface will guide you through each step!

Is HMP free to use?

+

Yes, HMP is completely free! We believe in making education management accessible to everyone. All features are available to both teachers and students without any hidden costs or premium tiers. This includes course creation, assignment management, progress tracking, and all future updates.

How do students join a course?

+

Joining a course is a straightforward process:
1. Log in to your student account
2. Click the "Join Course" button on your dashboard
3. Enter the course code provided by your teacher
4. Click "Join"
You'll immediately gain access to all course materials and assignments. The course code is automatically generated when teachers create a new course.
